Guidelines for Authors

  1. All Special Sessions will be held at the conference venue or online.
    1. To conduct a special session, the organizer must send a proposal containing:
      1. Aim & Scope/ Objectives
      2. Sub-topics of interest
      3. Contact information of the Organizer
      4. Organizers' short biography
      5. List of potential reviewers/ TPC members
      6. Tentative number of paper submissions
  2. Proposals will be evaluated by the TrustNet'26 Technical Committee and results shared via email.
  3. Organizers are encouraged to publicize Calls for Papers. Details of accepted sessions will appear on the conference website (https://trustnetcon.in/).
  4. Each paper requires at least two qualified reviews by the session organizers.
  5. Final decision will be taken by the Technical Committee after a third review.
  6. Acceptance rate and similarity index must be ≤30% and ≤15%, respectively.
  7. Minimum 5 accepted papers required per session; otherwise, sessions may be merged.

Important dates:

  • Notification of acceptance: At the earliest
  • Full-length paper submission and registration: As per Conference Timeline

The special session will be organized through online mode only.
